Andrianna has been a professional singer since the age of twelve when she performed and toured with the English National Opera. She's enjoyed global stadium tours as a backing singer for international acts including Boyzone, the Spice Girls, Michael Bolton, and Dodgy. She also writes and performs her own material and has released work with Ministry of Sound, Ramsey and Fen, Truesteppers and Acid Jazz.
